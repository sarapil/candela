## 🎯 Domain Analysis

**Candela** targets the **Food & Beverage (F&B)** industry — specifically Italian cafés, fine-dining restaurants, fast-casual eateries, cloud kitchens, and multi-branch restaurant chains. The platform covers the full restaurant lifecycle: public-facing digital presence (website, online ordering, reservations), back-office operations (POS, kitchen display, inventory, procurement), and business intelligence (daily closing, food cost reports, marketing analytics).
